SUMMER SCOUT CAMP

Summer Camp at Lost Pines Scout Reservation is one of the top experiences any Scout will have, enjoying a week-long adventure in the outdoors while making new friendships and deepening existing ones with other members of your Scouts BSA Troop. The memories made during camp remain with you for the rest of your life.

CAMP FEATURE

TENT CITY

Accommodation is well organized in a lovely tent city, where we can get to the nature as close as possible and forget about the internet and urban hustle and bustle.

TOURISM

Learning the basics of tourism is the most beloved part of the program. We learn how to prepare firewood, build a fire, extract water and navigate the stars.

CAMPFIRE MEALS

There are 2 types of food in the camp: regular food in bad weather conditions) and campfire meals (perhaps the most favorite dishes of all children).
